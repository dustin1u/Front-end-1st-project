Концепция Проекта

Introduction

1

This project is based on the creation of a website for those who need a momentary help about childrens’ needs, via question and answer service and booking service, also website works as a business page of the children's centre . Website uses an online database that stores all information entered into the system and is accessible from everywhere.Website is available to tutors and teachers and their customers, so that there will be no information leakage. Also the online database has real time crash statistics and performance monitoring to make the performance of the app more stable and update errors as quickly as possible.

The definition, study and analysis

Definition of the problem

Our client has a problem with the huge amount of paper used for distributing information about the centre, and also has a small problem with updating news and information about the centre instantly. Due to the fact that almost every record of news and events that happen in the centre are stored as paper or photo records, not every parent or client knows what's happening or who works where, there have been many copybooks and written papers collected that are taking up space in saves. That is why our client asked me to help with their problem.

The methods that are now in use

Our client uses Social media networks and paper handouts to share information with parents or children's representatives.

Methods

As of now internet and social media is not the best place to share personal information about staff and children’s achievements, paper handouts become waste and are thrown out most of the time.
